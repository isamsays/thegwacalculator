Why Your GWA Matters

Beyond grades, your General Weighted Average (GWA) plays a role in scholarships, honors, graduate school applications, and sometimes early-career hiring decisions.

Scholarships

Most scholarship programs in the Philippines specify a minimum GWA (commonly around 1.75 or better). Maintaining a competitive GWA improves access to financial support.

Honors and recognition

Graduation honors — Cum Laude, Magna Cum Laude, Summa Cum Laude — are typically awarded based on GWA thresholds and other institutional requirements.

Career & graduate study

While employers increasingly value skills and experiences, some still use academic metrics for early screening. A strong GWA can be a differentiator for new graduates.
