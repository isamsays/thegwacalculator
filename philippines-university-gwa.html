How Philippine Universities Use GWA

Universities in the Philippines may report grades and compute GPA/GWA differently. Knowing your university’s policies helps you interpret results accurately.

University of the Philippines (UP)

UP uses a numeric scale where 1.0 is the highest and 5.0 represents failure. Many colleges within UP set honors cutoffs (e.g., 1.75 for certain recognitions).

Ateneo de Manila University (ADMU)

Ateneo uses a QPI/letter grade system. For transfers or international comparisons the QPI is often mapped to local GWA equivalents.

De La Salle University (DLSU) & others

Private institutions adopt scales which might mirror 4.0 GPA or 5.0 GWA; always consult the student handbook or registrar for official policies.

Use the GWA Calculator as a consistent tool, then confirm the official conversion rules with your university when applying for formal processes.
