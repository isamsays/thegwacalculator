Understanding GPA and GWA

Both GPA (Grade Point Average) and GWA (General Weighted Average) are widely used to assess student performance, but their systems vary by region. For a broader breakdown, check our GPA GWA Converter Philippines 2025

What is GPA?

The Grade Point Average (GPA) is a U.S.-centric academic metric, generally on a scale of 0.00 to 4.00. It is calculated based on the number of credit hours and grade points received.

What is GWA?

The General Weighted Average (GWA) is typically used in the Philippines and parts of Asia. It's computed as the weighted average of grades, with 1.00 as highest and 5.00 as failing.

Sample Conversion Formula

A common method to estimate GWA from GPA is:

GWA = 5.00 - GPA

⚠️ This is a rough approximation. Always refer to your school's grading equivalency table.
