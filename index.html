How to Compute GWA in the Philippines

The General Weighted Average (GWA) is one of the most important numbers in a student’s academic journey. While our GWA Calculator does the heavy lifting, learning how to compute GWA manually helps you fully understand how grades are evaluated in Philippine schools.

Step-by-Step Formula

  1. Multiply the grade for each subject by its number of units.
  2. Add all the weighted grades.
  3. Divide the total weighted grades by the total number of units.

Example Computation

Suppose you took three subjects: - Math (3 units) – Grade 1.5 - English (3 units) – Grade 2.0 - Science (4 units) – Grade 1.75

Weighted Sum = (1.5 × 3) + (2.0 × 3) + (1.75 × 4) = 17.5 Total Units = 10 GWA = 17.5 ÷ 10 = 1.75
