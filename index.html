Why GWA matters

In the Philippines many universities use GWA to determine academic standing, qualifications for scholarships, and Latin honors (Cum Laude, Magna Cum Laude, Summa Cum Laude). GWA is a weighted metric that accounts for course units — this gives a truer picture of a student's performance than an unweighted average.

How the calculator works

Our calculator supports both 4.0 and 5.0 grading scales. If you studied on a 4.0 GPA scale, the tool applies a linear conversion to map your GPA to the 5.0 GWA convention commonly used in Philippine schools.
